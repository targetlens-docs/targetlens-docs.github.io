Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a setup fee?

No, there are no setup fees for any of our plans. You only pay the monthly subscription fee after your free trial period ends.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees.

Do you offer discounts for annual billing?

Yes, we offer a 15% discount for annual billing on all plans. Contact our sales team for more information.

What happens after my free trial ends?

After your 2-week free trial, you'll be prompted to select a pricing plan. Your account will not be automatically charged - you'll need to explicitly choose a plan to continue using TargetLens.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will be reflected in your next billing cycle.

Do you offer custom pricing for agencies?

Yes, we offer special agency pricing with the ability to manage multiple client accounts. Contact our sales team for more information.
