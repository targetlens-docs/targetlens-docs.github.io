Campaign Automation

TargetLens automates the entire campaign management process, from launch to optimization, while giving you full control to preview and edit before campaigns go live.

Managing digital marketing campaigns across multiple platforms is complex and time-consuming. Marketers often spend hours manually setting up campaigns, monitoring performance, and making adjustments—time that could be better spent on strategic initiatives.

TargetLens solves this challenge with end-to-end campaign automation that handles everything from campaign setup to ongoing optimization, while still giving you full control to review and edit before campaigns go live.

The Automation Process

1

Campaign Planning

TargetLens analyzes your marketing objectives and automatically develops a comprehensive campaign strategy.

2

Audience Selection

The system identifies the optimal audience segments to target based on your goals and budget.

3

Creative Generation

Personalized ad assets are created for each audience segment across all relevant platforms.

4

Human Review

You review and approve the campaign strategy, audience selections, and creative assets.

5

Campaign Launch

TargetLens automatically deploys your campaigns across all selected advertising platforms.

6

Continuous Optimization

AI continuously monitors performance and makes real-time adjustments to maximize results.

7

Performance Reporting

Comprehensive reports provide insights into campaign performance and ROI.

Key Features

Automated Campaign Setup

TargetLens handles all the technical aspects of campaign setup across multiple platforms, including:

  • Campaign structure creation
  • Budget allocation
  • Bid strategy configuration
  • Targeting parameters
  • Ad scheduling
  • Conversion tracking setup

Time Saved: Automated campaign setup typically saves marketing teams 5-10 hours per campaign launch.

Real-Time Optimization

TargetLens continuously monitors campaign performance and makes automatic adjustments to maximize results:

Optimization Type Description Frequency
Bid Adjustments Automatically adjusts bids based on performance data Hourly
Budget Reallocation Shifts budget to best-performing campaigns and ad sets Daily
Audience Refinement Narrows or expands audience targeting based on response Weekly
Creative Rotation Prioritizes best-performing ad creatives Daily
Dayparting Adjustments Optimizes ad scheduling based on performance by time Weekly

Human-in-the-Loop Control

While TargetLens automates the campaign process, you maintain full control:

  • Pre-Launch Review - Approve all campaign elements before going live
  • Edit Capabilities - Make changes to any aspect of the automated recommendations
  • Optimization Limits - Set parameters for automated adjustments
  • Manual Override - Take manual control of campaigns at any time
  • Approval Workflows - Multi-level approval process for enterprise teams

Automation Workflow

The TargetLens campaign automation workflow is designed to be simple and intuitive:

  1. Define Campaign Objectives - Specify your marketing goals, target audience, and budget
  2. Review AI Recommendations - TargetLens generates a comprehensive campaign strategy
  3. Approve or Edit - Review all elements and make any desired changes
  4. Launch Campaign - With one click, deploy your campaign across all platforms
  5. Monitor Performance - Track results in real-time through the TargetLens dashboard
  6. Review Optimizations - See all AI-driven adjustments and their impact

  • Optimization History - Record of all AI-driven optimizations and their impact
  • ROI Analysis - Comprehensive return on investment calculations
  • Trend Analysis - Performance trends over time with predictive forecasting
