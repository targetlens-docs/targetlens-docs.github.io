Getting Started with TargetLens

Welcome to TargetLens! This guide will walk you through the process of setting up your account and launching your first AI-powered marketing campaign.

Quick Start Guide

Getting started with TargetLens is simple and takes less than 5 minutes. Follow these steps to begin transforming your marketing with AI-driven audience targeting.

1

Sign Up for Beta Access

Visit targetlens.co and click on "Get Early Access" to join our beta program.

2

Create Your Account

Enter your basic company information and create your account credentials. No credit card is required for the beta program.

3

Connect Your Data Sources

Link your existing analytics, CRM, and social media accounts to enable TargetLens to analyze your audience data.

4

Define Your Marketing Objectives

Specify your marketing goals (conversion, brand awareness, etc.) to help TargetLens optimize for your specific needs.

5

Receive Your First Insights

Within 24 hours, TargetLens will deliver your first audience insights and targeting recommendations.

Note: During the beta program, you'll have full access to all TargetLens features for 2 weeks at no cost. After the trial period, you can choose a pricing plan that best fits your business needs.

System Requirements

TargetLens is a cloud-based platform that works with any modern web browser. There's no need to install any software on your computer.

Component Requirement
Web Browser Chrome 88+, Firefox 85+, Safari 14+, Edge 88+
Internet Connection Broadband (1 Mbps or higher)
Screen Resolution Minimum 1280 x 720
Devices Desktop, laptop, tablet (responsive design)

Account Setup

When setting up your TargetLens account, you'll need to provide some basic information about your business to help us tailor the platform to your needs.

Required Information

  • Company name and website - To identify your business
  • Industry - To provide industry-specific insights
  • Company size - To scale recommendations appropriately
  • Current marketing channels - To focus on your most important platforms
  • Marketing objectives - To align with your business goals

Optional Integrations

While not required, connecting the following platforms will significantly enhance TargetLens' ability to provide accurate audience insights:

  • Google Analytics - For website visitor behavior analysis
  • Facebook Business Manager - For social media audience insights
  • Google Ads - For advertising performance data
  • CRM system - For customer data analysis

Your First Campaign

After setting up your account and receiving your initial audience insights, you're ready to launch your first AI-powered marketing campaign.

Campaign Setup Process

  1. Review Audience Segments - Examine the audience segments identified by TargetLens and select the ones you want to target
  2. Set Campaign Parameters - Define your budget, timeline, and campaign objectives
  3. Review Ad Recommendations - TargetLens will generate personalized ad creative recommendations for each audience segment
  4. Edit and Approve - Make any desired changes to the ad creatives and approve them for launch
  5. Launch Campaign - TargetLens will automatically deploy your campaign across selected platforms
  6. Monitor Performance - Track your campaign's performance in real-time through the TargetLens dashboard
