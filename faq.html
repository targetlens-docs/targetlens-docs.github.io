Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about TargetLens, our AI-powered marketing platform for audience targeting and campaign automation.

General Questions

What is TargetLens?

TargetLens is an AI-powered marketing platform that helps brands discover their ideal audiences, optimize marketing ROI, create personalized ad assets, and automate campaign management. Our platform uses advanced neural networks and natural language processing to identify and target the most valuable audience segments for your business, resulting in higher conversion rates and lower acquisition costs.

How does TargetLens differ from other marketing platforms?

Unlike traditional marketing tools that focus on individual aspects of the marketing process, TargetLens provides an end-to-end solution that seamlessly integrates audience discovery, ROI optimization, ad creation, and campaign automation into a single platform. Our AI-driven approach delivers more precise audience targeting, more personalized creative, and more efficient campaign management than conventional tools.

Is TargetLens suitable for my business size?

TargetLens is designed to scale with businesses of all sizes. We offer different pricing tiers to accommodate startups, growing businesses, and enterprise organizations. Our platform delivers value regardless of your marketing budget by optimizing your existing spend for maximum returns.

Which industries does TargetLens work best for?

TargetLens is industry-agnostic and has delivered results for businesses across e-commerce, SaaS, consumer goods, financial services, healthcare, education, and many other sectors. Our AI models adapt to the specific characteristics and audience behaviors of your industry.

Is TargetLens available internationally?

Yes, TargetLens is available globally and supports marketing campaigns in all major markets. Our platform integrates with advertising platforms that operate internationally and can target audiences in multiple languages and regions.

Getting Started

How do I sign up for TargetLens?

During our beta phase, you can sign up for TargetLens by visiting our website and clicking on "Get Early Access." You'll be asked to provide some basic information about your business, and our team will reach out to set up your account. Once the platform launches publicly, you'll be able to sign up directly through our website.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 2-week free trial for all new users. During this period, you'll have full access to all platform features, allowing you to experience the benefits of TargetLens before committing to a subscription.

What information do I need to provide to get started?

To get the most out of TargetLens, we recommend providing:

  • Basic company information (name, website, industry)
  • Marketing objectives and KPIs
  • Access to your existing marketing platforms (optional but recommended)
  • Historical campaign data (optional but recommended)

The more information you provide, the more quickly TargetLens can deliver optimized results for your business.

How long does it take to set up TargetLens?

Basic setup can be completed in less than 30 minutes. If you choose to integrate with your existing marketing platforms, the process may take 1-2 hours depending on the number of integrations. Our customer success team is available to assist with the setup process and ensure a smooth onboarding experience.

Do I need technical expertise to use TargetLens?

No technical expertise is required to use TargetLens. Our platform is designed with a user-friendly interface that marketing professionals can navigate easily. For more advanced integrations or customizations, our support team is available to provide assistance.

Platform Features

How does TargetLens identify audience segments?

TargetLens uses a combination of neural networks, natural language processing, and behavioral pattern analysis to identify audience segments. The platform analyzes data from multiple sources, including:

  • Website visitor behavior
  • Social media engagement patterns
  • Purchase history and customer data
  • Third-party demographic and psychographic data
  • Competitive intelligence

This multi-dimensional analysis allows TargetLens to identify audience segments with unprecedented precision.

Can I edit the AI-generated ad creative?

Yes, TargetLens provides full editing capabilities for all AI-generated content. While our AI creates highly effective ad creative, we understand that you may want to make adjustments to align with specific brand guidelines or campaign objectives. Our platform allows you to edit copy, visuals, and formatting before campaigns go live.

Which advertising platforms does TargetLens integrate with?

TargetLens integrates with all major advertising platforms, including:

  • Facebook Ads
  • Instagram Ads
  • Google Ads
  • YouTube
  • LinkedIn Ads
  • Twitter Ads
  • TikTok Ads
  • Pinterest Ads
  • Amazon Advertising
  • Microsoft Advertising

  • Programmatic platforms (DV360, The Trade Desk, etc.)

We regularly add new platform integrations based on market trends and customer requests.

How does TargetLens optimize marketing ROI?

TargetLens optimizes marketing ROI through several mechanisms:

  • Cross-platform performance analysis to identify the most effective channels
  • Automated budget allocation to prioritize high-performing campaigns and audience segments
  • Bid strategy optimization based on conversion likelihood
  • Creative performance analysis to prioritize the most effective messaging
  • Continuous A/B testing to refine targeting and creative
  • Predictive modeling to forecast campaign performance

These optimizations work together to maximize the return on your marketing investments.

Can I maintain control over automated campaigns?

Absolutely. TargetLens follows a "human-in-the-loop" approach that gives you full control over your marketing campaigns. You can:

  • Review and approve all campaign elements before launch
  • Set parameters and limits for automated optimizations
  • Override AI recommendations when desired
  • Pause or modify campaigns at any time
  • Implement approval workflows for team collaboration

TargetLens automates the tedious aspects of campaign management while keeping you in control of your marketing strategy.

Data & Security

How does TargetLens handle my data?

TargetLens takes data security and privacy seriously. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption protocols. We maintain strict access controls and follow the principle of least privilege for all data access. Our platform is compliant with major data protection regulations, including GDPR and CCPA.

Does TargetLens share my data with third parties?

TargetLens does not sell or share your data with third parties for their own purposes. We only use your data to provide and improve our services. In some cases, we may use trusted third-party service providers to help us deliver specific aspects of our service, but these providers are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

Is TargetLens compliant with privacy regulations?

Yes, TargetLens is designed to be compliant with major privacy regulations, including GDPR, CCPA, and other regional data protection laws. We provide tools and features to help you maintain compliance with these regulations in your marketing activities, such as consent management and data subject access request handling.

What happens to my data if I cancel my subscription?

If you cancel your subscription, we retain your data for a limited period (typically 14 days) to allow for reactivation if you change your mind. After this period, your data is permanently deleted from our active systems. Backup copies may be retained for a longer period for disaster recovery purposes, but these are also eventually purged in accordance with our data retention policies.

Billing & Support

What payment methods do you accept?

TargetLens accepts all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) as well as ACH transfers for annual subscriptions. For enterprise customers, we also offer invoicing options with net-30 payment terms.

Can I change my subscription plan?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your subscription plan at any time through your account settings. Upgrades take effect immediately, with prorated charges for the remainder of your billing cycle. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

What kind of support does TargetLens provide?

TargetLens offers multiple support channels:

  • Email support for all customers
  • Live chat support during business hours
  • Phone support for Professional and Enterprise plans
  • Dedicated account manager for Enterprise plans
  • Comprehensive knowledge base and video tutorials

Our support team is available to assist with technical issues, strategic guidance, and best practices.

Do you offer training for new users?

Yes, all new customers receive access to our onboarding resources, including video tutorials, documentation, and webinars. Professional and Enterprise plans include personalized onboarding sessions with our customer success team. We also offer regular training webinars on specific platform features and marketing best practices.

What is your refund policy?

If you're not satisfied with TargetLens within the first 14 days of your paid subscription, you can request a prorated refund. After this initial period, we do not offer refunds for canceled subscriptions, but you can continue to use the service until the end of your current billing cycle.
